Welcome to Cape Verde

Cape Verde (Cabo Verde) is an archipelago and island country in the central Atlantic Ocean, consisting of ten volcanic islands. Known for its warm tropical climate, volcanic landscapes, delicious cuisine, and distinctive music like Morna, it's a destination unlike any other.

Activities & Adventures

Whether you seek relaxation or thrills, Cape Verde offers something for everyone:

  • Watersports: World-class conditions for windsurfing and kitesurfing, especially on Sal and Boa Vista. Diving and snorkeling reveal rich marine life.
  • Hiking: Explore dramatic volcanic peaks on Fogo, lush green valleys on Santo Antão, or coastal trails.
  • Culture & Music: Immerse yourself in the soulful sounds of Morna (a UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage), enjoy live music in Mindelo (São Vicente), and experience the local festivals.
  • Relaxation: Unwind on pristine white or black sand beaches and enjoy the laid-back atmosphere.

Island Highlights (Locations)

Sal

Famous for its golden sandy beaches like Santa Maria, salt pans, and watersports hubs.

Boa Vista

Known for vast sand dunes, turtle nesting sites, and stunning, less crowded beaches.

Santo Antão

A hiker's paradise with dramatic green mountains, deep ribeiras (valleys), and scenic trails.

Fogo

Dominated by the active volcano Pico do Fogo, offering unique landscapes and wine production.
